1. Installing the App on Your Phone

Add ShiftHub directly to your home screen with zero app store downloads

ShiftHub operates as a **Progressive Web Application (PWA)**. This means it is lightweight, launches instantly, works in full-screen standalone mode, and handles offline caching.

🍎 iPhone & iPad (Safari)

  1. Open **Safari** and navigate to your login URL.
  2. Tap the **Share button (📤)** on the bottom toolbar.
  3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
  4. Tap **"Add"** in the top right to complete.

🤖 Android Phone (Chrome)

  1. Open **Chrome** and navigate to your login URL.
  2. Look for a prompt at the bottom of your screen saying **"Add ShiftHub to Home Screen"**, or:
  3. Tap the **three vertical dots (⋮)** in the top-right corner.
  4. Tap **"Install App"** (or "Add to Home screen").
🔔

2. Enabling Push Notifications

Receive direct shift confirmations and coordinator alerts

It is critical to enable notifications so that you never miss a new shift assignment, calendar update, or message from your coordinator.

How to Activate Activating Alerts in 2 Taps

  1. Open your newly installed **ShiftHub** app from your mobile Home Screen.
  2. At the top right of the header, tap the **🔕 bell / speaker icon** (or look for the blue top-banner invitation).
  3. Tap the **"Enable"** button. When the browser prompts you to allow notifications, tap **"Allow"**.

* Note for iPhone users: iOS requires you to launch the app directly from your Home Screen (not inside regular Safari) to register push notifications.

3. Managing Your Shifts

Confirming assignments and claiming open sessions

ShiftHub splits shift management into two main tabs on your bottom menu bar:

📅 **Schedule Tab (Your Shifts)**

Displays all shifts specifically assigned to you. * **Confirm**: Tap a pending shift card and tap **Confirm** to lock in your availability. * **Release / Reject**: If you cannot make a session, tap **Release** to let your administrator know. You will be prompted to leave a brief, helpful note for the scheduler.

⚡ **Open Tab (Claimable Shifts)**

Lists all unassigned, available shifts posted by administrators. * **First-Come, First-Served**: If a session fits your schedule, tap **Claim** to assign yourself instantly. The shift moves directly to your personal schedule, and the admin is notified automatically!

💬

4. Core Application Features

Guide to messaging, news, calendar sync, forms, and resources

The bottom navigation bar grants you immediate access to your full work community:

  • 💬 **Real-Time Chat**: Direct message coordinators and open group chats with teacher cohorts. You can **long-press any message bubble** (or tap it) to open the mobile actions sheet to send quick emoji reactions, access the full emoji menu, reply directly, or forward messages. You can also delete your own messages. A single checkmark (✓) shows it has been sent, and a double checkmark (✓✓) shows it has been viewed by all recipients. Sent photos display as fast previews — tap any image to view its full size.
  • 📢 **Updates (Weekly Buzz)**: Access your franchise's newsletter and blog. Stay updated with drafts, leave comments, and like posts to engage with the office.
  • 🗓️ **Events RSVP**: View company training sessions or meetings, and select your status (**Going**, **Maybe**, **Can't Go**) so coordinators can track attendance.
  • 📝 **Forms Center**: Access questionnaires assigned to you by administrators. Fill out details securely and submit them directly from your phone (with built-in local saving backup).
  • 📚 **Resources Library**: Browse folders in the Knowledge Base to download lesson plans, forms, guidelines, and curriculum sheets on the go.
  • 👤 **Profile & Availability**: Update your personal info, profile photo, notification sounds, and device biometrics. You can also manage your availability: * **Weekly Slots**: Add recurring weekly time blocks when you are unavailable to work. * **Specific Dates Off**: Use the interactive calendar popup to toggle specific calendar days when you are unavailable (e.g. for doctor appointments or vacations). Schedulers will see these conflicts flagged on their roster. Finally, copy your personal **Calendar Sync URL** (webcal) directly into Google Calendar, Outlook, or Apple Calendar to sync confirmed shifts!
