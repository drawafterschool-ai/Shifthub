1. Initial Setup & Settings

Configure your franchise profile and business rules

Before scheduling classes, navigate to the Settings (⚙️) page in the left sidebar (desktop) or bottom tab bar (mobile) to populate your business rules:

  • Company Name: Customize your franchise name. This handles styling in outgoing emails, SMS messages, and PWA display headers.
  • Job / Session Types: Add custom types representing your classes (e.g. Teach, Assist, Workshop). Bind a default pay rate and a select theme color to each, color-coding the schedule automatically.
  • Payroll Hourly Tiers: Establish tier multipliers for teacher classifications. Pay summaries use these variables to calculate monthly payroll estimations.
📅

2. Creating & Managing Schedules

Build schedule grids, repeat series, and set open claimable shifts

The **Schedule** view features a month-by-month and week-by-week row layout designed for drag-free slot creation:

  • Auto-Populate Instructor: In the weekly grid view, click the plus (`+`) button on any instructor's row. The shift editor opens with that instructor pre-selected.
  • Recurring Shifts: Check "Repeat this shift" to duplicate assignments weekly. These are linked via a shared `seriesId` in Firestore, allowing you to edit or delete the entire sequence in bulk.
  • ⚡ Open Shifts (Claimable): Save a shift with the instructor set as *Unassigned*. It will appear on the Teacher PWA under "Open Shifts", allowing instructors to claim it. The administrator is notified to confirm or reject their claim.
  • Conflict Checks: The builder warns you if you assign a teacher to overlapping shifts. The conflict checking system ignores sibling shifts in the same recurrence series to prevent false-positives.
  • Confirmation Reset Rules: If you change the **date** or **time** on a shift previously confirmed by a teacher, its status resets to **Pending**, requiring the teacher to re-confirm. Changing minor details keeps it confirmed but notifies the teacher.

4. Team Directory & Onboarding

Manage teacher profiles and send secure invitation links

The **Directory** handles teacher files, contact coordinates, hourly tiers, and onboarding state:

  • Invitation Flow: Invite a new teacher by providing their email, phone, and hourly tier. ShiftHub creates their profile and generates a secure 72-hour password reset token link, which is emailed to them automatically.
  • First-Login Monitoring: On their first login, the PWA flags their onboarding state. Schedulers receive a push notification, and the Notifications page displays a "🎉 First Login" log with a quick "View profile" link.
💬

5. Chat Workspace & Group Customization

Coordinate in real-time and customize group channels

The **Chat** view has been upgraded with a scaled-up, mobile-responsive layout for quick group coordinate setups:

  • Group Avatars & Customizer: When creating a group chat via "New group message", you can select an emoji icon, choose a background color theme (8 choices), or upload a custom group image. Previews update in real-time.
  • Deep-Linked Welcome Alerts: New groups automatically write a system welcome message. This triggers a push notification to all members. Clicking the push notification opens the app and navigates directly to the target thread.
  • User Profile Avatars: Chat headers and message lists display user-uploaded profile pictures instead of basic letters.
  • Last Active Indicators: Direct message headers show the recipient's real-time last login timestamp (e.g. Last login: Yesterday at 4:12 PM) to check if your message has been received.
📝

6. Forms Center & Survey Dispatcher

Design form questionnaires and dispatch copies to teachers

The **Forms** tab is a digital questionnaire hub that allows admins to build and manage custom forms:

  • Form Builder: Compile forms with rich descriptions. Add single-line text inputs, textareas, dropdown selects, and checkboxes. Drag and drop field cards to re-order them.
  • Group Dispatch Checklist: Tap "Dispatch" on a template. A slide-up drawer allows you to select specific teachers, search by name, or click "Select All" to dispatch custom form copies in bulk.
  • Response Protection: The fields are snapshotted in the dispatch record, ensuring teacher responses remain unaffected if you edit the main template later.
  • Tracking Table & Viewer: View dispatched sheets, track progress (Pending in amber vs. Completed in green), and click any row to open the submitted answers in a structured modal reader.
📢

7. Weekly Buzz announcements

Publish team newsletters and track readership logs

The **Weekly Buzz** view is an internal announcements feed:

  • Rich Formatting: Use the inline editor to format announcements with headers, bullet points, bold text, and clickable links.
  • Custom Card Backdrops: Select background skins (e.g. Clouds, Snow, radial colors) to customize the post container.
  • Readership Logs: Track the readership with a progress bar (e.g., Seen by 6 / 10). Click "Show all" to see a roster showing which teachers have viewed the post.
  • Social Reactions: View likes and comment threads left by instructors at the bottom of the card.
🔔

8. Push Alerts & Mobile PWA Setup

Stay updated with push notifications and install the app on your phone

ShiftHub uses Web Push notifications to deliver native lock-screen alerts. Because notification permissions are device-specific, you must register each device:

📲 1. Install PWA on Phone

Open your admin URL (`/admin`) on your mobile browser.
• iOS Safari: Tap the Share button (📤) → "Add to Home Screen".
• Android Chrome: Tap Menu (⋮) → "Install App".
Open the app from your home screen.

🔔 2. Enable Notifications

Look for the status bell icon in the top-right header:
• `🔕` (Gray): Blocked/inactive. Tap it to open the setup banner.
• `🔔` (Green): Active.
• `📲` (Gray): Standalone mode required (iOS Safari).
Tap **Enable** to grant permission.

* Pulsing red `"NEW"` badges appear on navigation items in both the admin sidebar and teacher toolbar when new updates, resource nodes, or unconfirmed shifts require attention.
